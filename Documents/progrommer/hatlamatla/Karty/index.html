EVERYTHING

*except everything

Everything is a work created by combining the famed "Polemics of life" by Caltan & Guide, with the "Book of the Dead", a book containing the stories of all dead, "Journal" by the Wanderer and many other famed works. Everything is a structured compilation of all that has passed, is passing, and what possibly will.

CHARACTERS

Mr. Meowtz

A cat. Just a simple cat. However, even the world ending monsters fear it for some reason...

Money

The richest man in the world. Publicaly, he owns half the universe. In truth... No one knows.

Pyro

A crazy, genius pyromancer that came from a book. Excels in explosions and fiery pranks, but his pancakes are inedible.

Frost

Once an ice king, now an ice cream king. Always composed and dignified, even when poisoning people to death with ice cream.

Guide

The one who guides the souls of the dead (and living) towards eternal rest, he enjoys hearing their stories.

Death

Death himself. A friendly guy, likes tea and cookies. Only kills those who deserve it... And thats why everyone's dead.

The Forgotten king

In the past, a glorious king of a prosperous nation. In the present, a broken, lonely man slowly dying within the shambles of his land. Likes tragic poems.
